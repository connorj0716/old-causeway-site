Our Story

A century on the causeway, faithfully reimagined.

The original Old Causeway Inn opened in 1914 as the bayside stop where bay men, clammers and travelers shared a meal and a story at the gateway to LBI. Decades later, we picked up where they left off — pairing the warmth of an old-time tavern with the craft of a modern steak and oyster house.

The Experience

Three pleasures, perfected daily.

N° 01

Wood-Fired Steaks

Filet, NY Strip, Ribeye and Prime Rib — grilled over open flame, finished with bone marrow butter or your choice of seven house sauces.
N° 02

The Raw Bar

Barnegat Bay clams, fresh-shucked oysters, jumbo shrimp cocktail and our showstopping seafood towers.
N° 03

Sunday Brunch

The Mud City Benedict, brioche French toast and bottomless mimosas — Sundays 10 AM to 1 PM.
