On-Site Private Events

Your night, on the bay.

From bayside rehearsal dinners to corporate buy-outs, our private spaces are designed for the kinds of nights you'll talk about for years. Three event rooms, a full-service patio, and a dedicated events team that handles the details — start to finish.

  • Rehearsal dinners & welcome parties
  • Corporate dinners & full buyouts (up to 220)
  • Bridal & baby showers
  • Holiday parties & milestone celebrations
  • Memorial luncheons
  • Custom menus, A/V, and dedicated event captain

Off-Site Catering

The Causeway, delivered.

From clambakes on the beach to holiday spreads at the office — our catering team brings the full Old Causeway lineup to wherever you're gathering. Raw bar, seafood towers, signature steaks, half-tray entrees, sliders, salads and dessert by the platter.

  • Raw Bar & seafood tower setups
  • Hot & cold entrees by the half-tray
  • Slider stations & bar bites
  • Salad boards & sides
  • Dessert platters & coffee service
  • Full equipment rental available
Heads up — All catering orders require 24 hours advance notice. Dessert orders require 48 hours. Pricing and items subject to change.
